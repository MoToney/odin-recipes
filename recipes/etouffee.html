Etouffee

Description

Etouffee done right will be the go to in your household. The creamy cajun flavors are truly TO DIE FOR.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Cream of Mushroom
  • Butter
  • Seasoning
  • Rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Diced Tomato

Steps

  1. Cook Shrimp with butter
  2. Season Well
  3. Add Veggies
  4. Add Diced Tomato
  5. Add Add Cream of Mushroom
  6. Add Rice to Bowl
  7. Add Scoop of Etouffee
  8. Enjoy
