Burger

Description

A homeade burger can be very versitile. They're perfect for gamedays, cookouts, or just a quick and healthier alternative to fast food.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Seasoning
  • Pickle
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Season Patty
  2. Cook Patty
  3. Add Lettuce
  4. Add Tomato
  5. Add Pickle
  6. Add Ketchup
  7. Close Burger
  8. Enjoy
