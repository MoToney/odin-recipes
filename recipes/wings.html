Wings

Description

Wings are a staple in many househoulds. Wings feature flats and drums from your bird of choice, typically chicken is used. They come in variety of dishes and have a variety of tastes. I personally prefer lemon pepper and garlic parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Seasonings
  • Binder
  • Sauce
  • Ranch(Optional)

Steps

  1. Clean Wings
  2. Dry Wings
  3. Apply Binder
  4. Season Accordingly
  5. Cook for 28 Minutes
  6. Flip and cook for 25 Minutes
  7. Prepare Sauce
  8. Apply Sauce
  9. Enjoy
